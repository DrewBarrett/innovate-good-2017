About UMBC Watch Dawg

Our Mission:

This web application is made for UMBC students and visitors to document and report crimes that happened on campus. Our mission is to provide justice for all crimes and make UMBC a safer place. When the police are first contacted, the witness does not provide all of the infromation and are later questioned when the police arrive. This web application is designed to help the witness remeber what actually happened before they are influenced by others or are influenced with time.

Facts:

-50% of crimes go unreported

-More than 2,000 false convictions in past 23 years

-Witnesses can be subject to memory distortions that can alter their account of events

-Only a portion of the memor of the incident is tranfered from short term memory to long term (permanent) memory

Application Usage

If you are a victim or witness of a crime the first thing that you should do is call the police. This web application is to be used to document the details of the crime that police may ask you questions about later. It is crucial to document all of the details and events of the time because witnesses start to forget and change details of the event only minutes after the incident. Eye wtinesses often are influenced by other people so it is important to document all of the details before you are influenced or forget.
